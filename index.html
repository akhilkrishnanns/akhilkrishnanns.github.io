Namanattu Kudumbakshethram

Namanattu Kudumbam (Namanattu family) is a very ancient family. It is situated near to A.K.G. Junction, Parayakadu, Kuthiathod Village, Cherthala Taluk, Alappuzha District, in the State of Kerala. According to the available information, the Namanattu family had got very old age of decades in a Survey conducted 100 years back. The present generation adores the deities which were kept and adored by the ancient generations of Namanattu family.
